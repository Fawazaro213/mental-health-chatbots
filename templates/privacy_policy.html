1. Information We Collect

MindCare collects the following types of information when you use our services:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • University counseling services (with your consent)
  • Emergency services if risk of harm is detected
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
