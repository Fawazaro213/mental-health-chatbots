1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. MindCare uses cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in during your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our services

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Core functionality (login, security) Session
Preference Remember language/accessibility settings 30 days
Analytics Improve our services (anonymous) 1 year

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (disable/all/delete)
  • Our cookie consent banner when first visiting
  • Third-party opt-out tools for analytics

Note: Disabling essential cookies may break site functionality.
